# 📱 You're Offline

Don't worry! lnd-boilerplate is designed to work offline.

## 🔄 What's Available Offline

- **Cached Pages**: Previously visited pages are available
- **Core Features**: Basic functionality continues to work
- **Offline Actions**: Your interactions are saved for later sync

## 📱 PWA Features

This app can be installed on your device for a native-like experience:

- **Home Screen**: Add to your device's home screen
- **Offline Access**: Use without internet connection
- **Push Notifications**: Get updates when you're back online
- **Background Sync**: Actions sync automatically when online

## 🚀 Getting Back Online

When your connection is restored:

1. **Auto-sync**: Offline actions will sync automatically
2. **Fresh Content**: Latest content will be fetched
3. **Real-time Updates**: Live features will resume

## 💡 Offline Tips

- **Bookmark Important Pages**: They'll be cached for offline use
- **Use Offline Actions**: Comment, like, and interact normally
- **Check Network Status**: Look for the offline indicator

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

If you're having issues:

- **Refresh the Page**: Sometimes helps with cached content
- **Clear Cache**: Remove old cached data if needed
- **Check Settings**: Ensure PWA features are enabled

## 📞 Support

Need help? Check our [Documentation](/docs) or [Contact Us](/about) when you're back online.
